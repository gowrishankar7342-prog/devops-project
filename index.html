// capabilities

Built for teams
who ship daily.

Every feature was designed around one constraint: production is sacred. Speed is the reward for getting safety right.

⚡
Parallel Execution
Independent stages run simultaneously on elastic runners. A 12-minute pipeline becomes 3 minutes at scale.
↩️
Instant Rollback
One command or one click. Previous image versions are kept ready. Mean time to recover under 45 seconds.
🌐
Multi-Cloud Deploy
AWS, GCP, Azure, and bare-metal. A single pipeline definition works everywhere with environment-specific overrides.
📊
DORA Metrics
Deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, and MTTR tracked automatically. No extra instrumentation.
🔐
Secrets Management
Vault-native integration. Secrets are injected at runtime, never stored in CI logs or environment variables.
🔔
Smart Alerts
Alert routing based on ownership. The team who owns the code gets paged, not the whole org. SLO-aware suppression.
